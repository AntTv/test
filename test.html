Heroes for Hannah

Schools VS TR Heroes

Friday, June 9

7:00 p.m.

Pine Belt Arena

Tickets available starting on April 27 at Ticketmaster.

A little over six months ago life changed dramatically for Hannah and her family. After being involved in a very serious car accident, her family and friends rallied around her and the Donner family to help however possible in the wake of their nightmare.

As Hannah continues to recover, her medical expenses continue to rise. We hope you will join us for an exciting basketball game between police and fire department members and the students, teachers and coaches from Central Regional High School and Toms River High School North in an effort to help this wonderful family with Hannah's medical expenses.
